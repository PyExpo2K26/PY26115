Disaster preparedness guide

Clear, local steps to stay safe before, during, and after flooding.

Community safety checklist
01

Before flood

Prepare early to avoid last-minute risk.

  • Store drinking water for at least 3 days.
  • Prepare an emergency kit with torch, radio, and batteries.
  • Move valuables and documents to higher levels.
  • Charge phones and keep power banks ready.
  • Save local helpline numbers and family contacts.
02

During flood

Prioritize safety and follow official guidance.

  • Avoid walking or driving through moving water.
  • Turn off electricity and gas if water enters the home.
  • Move to higher ground or upper floors.
  • Follow evacuation instructions from authorities.
  • Keep a radio or phone updates for alerts.
03

After flood

Recover carefully and reduce health risks.

  • Avoid contaminated water and disinfect surfaces.
  • Check structural damage before re-entering rooms.
  • Document damage with photos for claims.
  • Dry and ventilate to prevent mold growth.
  • Boil water until local supply is declared safe.
